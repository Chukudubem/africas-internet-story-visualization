AFRICA'S INTERNET STORY




PROVENANCE

The data for this visualization can be found on the UNdata website. It however was originally sourced from the International Telecommunications Union(ITU), the main source of global, and internationally comparable, telecommunication/ICT statistics. Our purpose is to shed some light on the growth of the world wide web since its invention in 1990 - using Africa as a use-case. We aim to visually communicate the development process within the continent and compare with five(5) countries from the rest of the world.
